(Switch Accessible) Mancala

Instructions: Press space key to look at all the options, enter to select

RULES AND REMINDERS:

Your moves will happen counter-clockwise

If you land in your own bank (the one on the right), you get another turn!

If your last stone lands in an empty space, you will add the contents of that pit as well as the pit across from yours to your bank!

You will be playing against the computer! Good luck!
